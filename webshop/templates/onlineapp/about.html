About us

G3-Sports Group consists of the concepts G3-Sports and G3-Sports Outlet, which together have about 180 physical stores and each e-commerce. Together they form Sweden's largest sports and sports fashion chain. Stockholm is home to the G3-Sports Group's head office, which is called the “service office”, as it aims to support all stores and customer service. The company is owned by the Group-3 Lexicon (Python/IT Security) 2022 batch and operates in Sweden, Finland and Norway.

G3-Sports was the original concept that was launched January 2022 and currently also contains a number of specialty stores such as G3-Sports Ski and G3-Sports Plus. G3-Sports Outlet offers lower prices than the market in general thanks to larger batch purchases..

Since 2022, G3-Sports Sports Camp has been organized for children and young people. G3-Sports Sports Camp has its own organization and is run as an SVB company (special dividend limitation), which means that G3-Sports Group reinvests and distributes all profits to participating associations. Approximately 7,000 children are activated annually during the camp weeks.

With a vision of activating the world, G3-Sports wants to inspire an active life for everyone.
